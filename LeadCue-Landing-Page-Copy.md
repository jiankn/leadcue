## 11. FAQ

### Is LeadCue an email finder?

No. LeadCue may extract public emails and contact pages from websites, but its main job is to find sales cues and outreach angles from website signals.

### Does LeadCue scrape LinkedIn?

No. The MVP does not scrape LinkedIn or Sales Navigator.

### Can I send cold emails from LeadCue?

No. LeadCue helps with prospect research and first-line generation. You can export leads into your existing outreach tool.

### Who is LeadCue built for?

LeadCue is built first for SEO agencies, web design agencies, marketing agencies, and B2B founders who do outbound prospecting.

### Does LeadCue store the websites I analyze?

LeadCue stores your saved prospect cards. Raw website snapshots are minimized and used only to generate your analysis.

### Will there be an AppSumo deal?

Possibly. The beta will launch first, then an AppSumo lifetime deal may be offered with monthly scan limits.
